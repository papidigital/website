Case Study

Record-Breaking Podcast Marketing - The Donnie Brasco Story

Julian Espinosa / December 2020

Overview

This podcast is centered around Joe Pistone who was the undercover FBI agent that infiltrated the New York Mafia in the late 70s. The goal was to test the market and determine the viability of this podcast. Through this test, we discovered the audience and the type of content that appeals to users.

The results of this test were shocking. Not only did we discover an audience but this campaign broke paid ad records for podcasting. This test sets how podcast marketing will be done in the future. This also happens to a really cool podcast, listen to it if you get a chance!

Market research involved, and how we determined our target audience

We used Audience Insights to find the audience. It was a lot of trial and error. We searched for mafia-related interests to see if Facebook had an audience for it. We searched for law enforcement interests.

Based on several types of approaches we made a “hypotheses” that targeting mob-related movies would be the best performer (it was only an educated guess). We based our selection around 90s mafia movies. We assumed they would have likely seen “Donnie Brasco.”
